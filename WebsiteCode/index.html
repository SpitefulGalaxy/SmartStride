About SmartStride

This device is dedicated to advancing rehabilitation for individuals with Idiopathic Toe Walking (ITW) by enhancing daily interaction between patients and healthcare professionals. Our device features a compression sock embedded with EMG and IMU sensors, allowing for real-time monitoring of a patientâ€™s walk to provide clinicians with continuous, precise data on progress and ITW evaluation. By utilizing machine learning, our device classifies ITW severity on a sliding scale, allowing for more targeted and effective care. Data from the device integrates seamlessly into this user-friendly web application, accessible to both clinicians and patients, supporting early problem identification and potentially reducing recovery and rehabilitation time.
