About SmartStride

Smart Stride is a group of five seniors working on their final capstone project. This design and engineering challenge with a health focus was chosen to address a challenge in the medical community. Currently, in the world of telehealth, the expansions of sensors in at-home technologies continue to grow. This device sits right alongside your at-home blood pressure or heart monitor, allowing for patients to categorize and collect data about their individual condition individually. With SmartStride, weâ€™re revolutionizing the rehabilitation journey for individuals with Idiopathic Toe Walking (ITW) through advanced technology and data-driven insights. Our device, a compression sock embedded with EMG and IMU sensors, enables continuous monitoring of gait, offering healthcare providers a detailed view of patient progress. By using machine learning to classify ITW severity on a sliding scale, SmartStride delivers tailored, real-time data, helping clinicians make informed adjustments to treatment plans. The smart device truly creates a difference in the lives of patients with this condition..

The ITW Assessment Device

Our device is a cutting-edge solution designed to transform the rehabilitation process for individuals with Idiopathic Toe Walking. By leveraging advanced technology, we're able to provide unprecedented insights into patient progress and treatment effectiveness.

Key Features:

  • Electromyography (EMG) capabilities
    • Allows for in-depth muscle monitoring
    • Pinpoint muscular activation technology
  • Easy session based data collection
    • Automatic data synchronization
    • Easy to use interface
    • Allows for easy data collection sessions
  • Remote assessment capabilities for improved accessibility
    • Web-based monitoring allows your doctor to see and give feedback without the need of an in-person visit
    • Cloud-based data storage
    • Secure patient data transmission
  • Machine learning for severity classification
    • Accurate ITW severity assessment
  • Wearable and comfortable design
    • Lightweight and non-intrusive sensors

Our Mission

At SmartStride, we're committed to addressing the limitations of traditional rehabilitation methods. Our goal is to provide more effective and accessible treatment options for ITW patients through:

  • Personalized assessment and treatment plans
  • Remote monitoring capabilities
  • Data-driven insights for healthcare providers
  • Improved patient outcomes through continuous feedback

Collaborative Innovation
